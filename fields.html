🏗️ Fields of Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is divided into different fields, each focusing on a specific area of infrastructure and development. These fields work together to create safe, efficient, and long-lasting structures.

  • Structural Engineering – focuses on designing buildings, bridges, and other structures to make sure they are strong and stable.
  • Transportation Engineering – deals with roads, highways, traffic systems, and transportation planning to improve mobility and safety.
  • Environmental Engineering – focuses on protecting the environment by managing waste, water treatment, and pollution control.
  • Geotechnical Engineering – studies soil and rock behavior to ensure strong foundations for buildings and structures.
  • Water Resources Engineering – manages water supply systems, dams, and flood control to ensure safe and reliable water use.

Each field plays an important role in improving infrastructure and ensuring that communities function properly and safely.
